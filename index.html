Recommendations

Places

for those who love warm places, I highly recommend Aruba, it’s a small island with very nice shops, and the ocean is incredibly clear and blue, it’s a breathtaking scene. I will always recommend trying to save up and plan a trip to Europe at least once in your life, as an example Italy and Germany, the statues and architecture of antique buildings with such importance are something I’d love to see again for the first time.

Books

I will forever and always recommend classics. Sure, they may be a little hard to comprehend at first since it’s a more elegant and old writing style (descriptive, bla bla bla), but the emotions and scenery are always incredibly well written, sticks with you forever no matter if theyre a century old or just from a decade ago. I'd recommend Before The Coffee Gets Cold, its short and includes a total of 4 stories based on time-traveling to change the past, it has a way to get you hooked and sentimental.

Movies/Series

I’m a big fan of analog and psychological horror series and movies. It portrays a better-made horror genre that not only keeps you waiting, but highly focused hence you’ll never know what could come by to the screen. I’d recommend movies such as The Crimson Peak, Eli or Midsommar (these are very gorey, please check warnings before even considering on watching.)
